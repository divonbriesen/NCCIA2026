## General Session Format

All sessions follow a similar structure:

1. **Introductions** - Meet participants and establish context
2. **Background/Summary** - Set the stage for the session content
3. **Read the Room** - Assess participant experience and adjust accordingly
4. **Core Content** - Session-specific activities and demonstrations
5. **Wrap-up** - Summarize key takeaways
6. **Questions/Suggestions/Follow-Up** - Address participant needs and next steps
